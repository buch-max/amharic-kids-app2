BACKGROUND MUSIC PLACEHOLDER

Please place a child-friendly background music file named "kids-music.mp3" in this directory.

For royalty-free music options, consider:
1. Free music from royalty-free sites like Pixabay, Bensound, or Free Music Archive
2. Children's music with appropriate licensing
3. Traditional Ethiopian children's music if available

Recommended characteristics:
- Gentle, upbeat tempo
- Child-friendly melodies
- No lyrics or simple, educational lyrics
- Duration: 2-5 minutes (it will loop automatically)
- MP3 format, moderate quality (128-192kbps)

The music button allows users to play/pause the music, and their preference will be saved in their browser.
